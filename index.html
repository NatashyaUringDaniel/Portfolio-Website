About

Hello, welcome to my personal portfolio. My name is Natashya Uring Daniel, I'm 21 years old. I was born in Miri on 29th June 1999. When i was in primary 6, i moved to Kuching with my family and from there i started my new journey. In Kuching, i started with my new hobby which is to become a blogger and a photographer.

During my leisure time at home, i mostly spending my time creating my own blogs. Through blogs, i love to express my feelings when no one was there for me, i love to share my knowledge and my experienced how i achieve my goals and overcome the obstacles in life.

Creating blog is a good idea because i can earn money from blogging while imrpove my writing skills. The more we write, the better we become at it. Most professional bloggers write on a regular basis and therefore analyze and proofread their writing constantly. We also may even get feedback from readers to help us become a better writer.

I am currently studying at UiTM Samarahan, Sarawak. During my life time in University, I took a lot of photos during my leisure time. The reason why i love took photos because photos tilt my memories toward the good experiences i've had. I always trying to figure out ways to hold on to memories. One thing I do is keep a daily one-sentence journal. I don't have the time or energy to write much every day, but I can manage to jot down one sentence.

Photographs are another easy way to record little moments that are precious but easily forgotten. Besides, photography brings me closer to natural and spirituality. Have a look around at the wonders of nature. Whether someone believe in God or not, we can’t help but feel a stirring at the beauty surrounding us. We simply can’t help but understand how very small we are while we are realizing how very vast everything else is.

Photographs play an important role in everyone’s life because they connect us to our past, it remind us of people, places, feelings, and stories.

A picture keeps a thousand memories.
